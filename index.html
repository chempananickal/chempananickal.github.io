About

I'm a dual student and code monkey working at Sanofi. I've mostly worked in the fields of bioinformatics and chemoinformatics, and I've mostly just coded in R and Python. I know my way around the RDKit, and my usual problem solving strategies involve throwing a lot of graph theory at the problem until it goes away. And yes, it's worked out pretty well so far.

This site is for writing, project notes, and interactive tools as I build things out. The blogs will be about anything that I seem to waste my time on at the moment, so don't expect anything consistent.
